Lesson 1: Understanding Financial Basics

In this lesson, we will cover the basic principles of financial literacy:

  • What is financial literacy? Learn how understanding money management is key to financial success.
  • Income and Expenses Understand how income and expenses affect your financial position.
  • Savings and Investments Learn about savings accounts, stocks, and other forms of investments.
  • Budgeting Learn how to create and stick to a budget.

Important Terms:

  • Assets: Anything that you own and has value.
  • Liabilities: Debts or obligations that you owe to others.
  • Net Worth: The difference between your assets and liabilities.
